Berry Smoothie Bowl

Ingredients

☺ 1 c Frozen Mixed Berries

☺ 1/2 c Almond Milk

☺ 2 tbsp Peanut Butter

☺ 1/4 c granola

☺ 1/2 c Greek Yogurt

☺ 1 tsp Cinnamon

Directions

Begin by gathering your ingredients. Take out a blender and add your frozen berries and almond milk and blend until smooth. If you're having trouble blending, you can always add more almond milk to make it easier to blend. Once blended to a smooth consistency, add to a bowl with greek yogurt. Top with granola, peanut butter, and any other toppings you'd like and enjoy!
