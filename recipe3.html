Warm Caesar Salad

Ingredients

☺ 1 c Kale

☺ 1/2 c Sweet Potato

☺ 1/2 Cherry Tomatoes

☺ 1/2 c Rice

☺ 2 tbsp Parmesan

☺ 2 tbsp Caesar Dressing

☺ 1/2 tsp Pepper

☺ 1/2 tsp Garlic

☺ 1/2 tsp Salt

Directions

Chop sweet potatoes into one inch cubes, then mix in meduim sized bowl with olive oil, pepper, garlic powder, salt, and red pepper flakes. Spread potatoes evenly on pan and place oven in oven set to 400 degrees.

Begin by sauteeing the kale in oil, salt, and pepper on low to medium heat Add in two tablespoons of water in order to wilt the kale. Add in the premade rice, tomatoes, and sweet potatoes. Once everything is warmed up place in a bowl and top with parmesan and caesar dressing.
