Avocado Toast

Ingredients

☺ 1/2 of 1 Avocado

☺ 1-2 Slices of Bread

☺ 1-2 Eggs

☺ 1/2 tsp Salt

☺ 1/2 tsp Pepper

☺ 1/2 tsp Garlic

☺ 1 tbsp Everything But The Bagel Seasoning

Directions

Begin by getting all of your ingredients out.

Place your small to medium sized pan on the stove and turn the burner on low to medium heat. Start with low and if it's taking too long, turn it up. I usually cook my eggs somewhere in between low and medium heat. Put your eggs in the pan and every 30 seconds use a pushing motion with the spatula. If your eggs start to pile up, grab the handle of the pan and lightly shake it forawrd and back. repeat these steps until the eggs are done to your liking and set aside.

Next, put your bread in the toaster. While your bread is toasting, cut your avocado in half, spoon out the avocado and begin smashing with a fork. Add in your garlic, salt, and pepper to the avocado. Then, begin slicing your tomatoes. You can simply slice them in half, or cut them into two or more slices. Once all of your ingredients are ready, spread the avocado on the toast. Then, sprinkle on your Everything but the Bagel Seasoning and add your tomatoes on top. Once your tomatoes are added, place your eggs on the bread. To finish, top with Sriracha and some more Everything but the Bagel Seasoning. And viola, you did it!
