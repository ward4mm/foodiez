Foodiez

Hi, my name is Melissa & welcome to Foodiez!

When I first started living on my own in college I never knew what to cook, grocery shopping was a nightmare, and I was always wasting food. Cooking seemed to take up way too much time and soon I was only eating coffee, a protein bar, and whatever I could scrounge up for dinner. My goal with foodies is to show other college kids that cooking can be fun, fast, and easy! Check out my "Recipes" page for breakfast, lunch, and dinner ideas and my "Articles" page for ways to make cooking more fun, fast, and sustainable!
